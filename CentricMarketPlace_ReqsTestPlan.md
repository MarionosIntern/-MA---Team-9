# Centrix Marketplace Requirements Testing

## Actors 
- Provider P: Seller
- Customer C: Customer
- Service S: Product 

## Use Cases
#### 1. Provider - Create Provider profile (sign up as a seller):
1. Seller P1 creates a profile when logging in the first time.

#### 2. Provider - Create product for product listing (upload a product for customers):
1. P1 uploads a product S1 with values for searchable criteria A1, A2, A3 (A1=Name, A2=Price, A3=Category). P1 exits the app.

#### 3. Customer - Create customer profile (sign up as customer)
1. Customer C1 creates a profile after logging in for the first time.

#### 4. Customer - View product listing & Purchases product (buying from seller)
1. Customer C2 logs in for the first time and creates a new profile.
2. C2 views product S1 from P1.
3. C2 purchases S1.

#### 5. Customer - Write review (Rate a product):
1. C2 logs in and writes a positive review of S1. C2 logs off.

#### 6. Customer - Modify Profile:
1. C1 logs in and modifies thier profile.
2. C1 exits.

#### 7. Provider - Reply to reviews & MOdify Profile:
1. Seller P1 logs in and reads their review on product S1 and replies with "Thanks".
2. P1 modifies their profile. P1 exits.
